Learning About Nature and Trees

Types of Trees

Oak Tree

Oak trees are strong and can live for hundreds of years. They produce acorns and provide homes for wildlife.

Maple Tree

Maple trees are famous for their bright red and orange leaves during autumn.

Pine Tree

Pine trees stay green all year and have needle-shaped leaves.

Why Trees Are Important

  • They produce oxygen
  • They provide shade
  • They clean the air
  • They provide shelter for animals
